Complete Rulebook

We operate on a "Semi-Anarchy" philosophy. You are free to play exactly how you want—as long as you aren't cheating or being a terrible person in chat. Read the details below.

1. The "Anything Goes" Gameplay Mechanics

  • Griefing is 100% Allowed: If you find a base, it is yours to destroy, raid, or take over. There are no land claims. Hide your coordinates well, travel far, and build secure defenses.
  • PvP & Killing: Player versus Player combat is enabled everywhere outside of spawn. You can kill anyone for their loot, for revenge, or just because you want to.
  • Scamming & Deception: In-game betrayals, trade scams, and lying to other players are all part of the game. Trust no one.
  • Trapping: Trap bases, portal traps, and TP (Teleport) trapping are completely allowed. Enter portals and accept teleport requests at your own risk.

2. Zero Tolerance for Cheating

  • Hacked Clients: Using clients like Meteor, Wurst, Aristois, or any similar software that alters vanilla mechanics is a permanent ban. This includes Killaura, Aimbot, Freecam, Auto-Totem, and Flight.
  • X-Ray: Using X-Ray texture packs, mods, or mining using external seed maps to locate ores, bases, or dungeons will result in a ban.
  • Auto-Clickers: Hardware macros, software auto-clickers, or "putting a weight on your mouse" for combat is cheating. (Using them specifically while AFK at a mob farm is allowed, but not in PvP).
  • Allowed Mods: You are allowed to use performance mods (Optifine, Sodium, Lithium), visual mods (Shaders, Fullbright), minimaps (ONLY if entity/player radar is disabled), and schematic mods (Litematica, provided the "EasyPlace/Printer" function is OFF).

3. Exploits, Glitches & Lag

  • Item Duplication: Duping valuable items (diamonds, shulkers, armor) using glitches or chunk overloading is strictly banned and will result in a wipe of your inventory and a ban.
  • TNT / Carpet Duping: We understand the technical Minecraft community. Duping TNT for world-eaters or carpets for fuel is generally accepted.
  • Intentional Lag Machines: Building redstone contraptions with the sole purpose of crashing the server or lagging out other players will result in an immediate, permanent IP ban.
  • Entity Cramming: Keep your farms reasonable. Stacking 500 cows or villagers into a single chunk ruins the server TPS (Ticks Per Second) for everyone else. Staff may clear excessive entities without warning.

4. Chat Conduct ("Don't Be Mean")

  • Hate Speech & Discrimination: We do not tolerate racism, sexism, homophobia, slurs, or extreme forms of harassment. Trash talking in PvP is fine, but crossing the line into targeted hate will get you removed.
  • Real-Life Threats & Doxxing: Threatening someone's real-life safety, threatening to DDoS/Swat them, or releasing a player's personal real-world information (Doxxing) is a permanent, unappealable ban.
  • Spam & Advertising: Do not spam the chat with repeated messages, excessive caps, or ASCII art. Advertising other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or personal links is not allowed.
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be an Admin, Moderator, or famous Content Creator to extort or deceive other players will result in a severe punishment.
