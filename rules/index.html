Complete Rulebook

We operate on a "Semi-Anarchy" philosophy. You are free to play exactly how you want—as long as you aren't cheating or being toxic.

1. The "Anything Goes" Mechanics

  • Griefing is 100% Allowed: If you find a base, it is yours to destroy, raid, or take over. Hide your coordinates well.
  • PvP & Killing: Player versus Player combat is enabled everywhere outside of spawn. You can kill anyone for any reason.
  • Scamming & Deception: In-game betrayals, trade scams, and lying to other players are all part of the game.
  • Trapping: Trap bases, portal traps, and TP trapping are completely allowed. Enter portals at your own risk.

2. Zero Tolerance for Cheating

  • Hacked Clients: Using clients like Meteor or Wurst that alter vanilla mechanics is a permanent ban.
  • X-Ray: Using X-Ray texture packs or seed maps to locate ores/bases will result in a ban.
  • Approved Mods: Performance mods (Optifine, Sodium), visual mods (Shaders), and schematic mods (without printer) are fine.

3. Exploits, Glitches & Lag

  • Item Duplication: Duping valuable items using glitches is banned and will result in an inventory wipe and ban.
  • TNT / Carpet Duping: Duping TNT for world-eaters or carpets for fuel is generally accepted.
  • Intentional Lag Machines: Building contraptions to crash the server is an immediate IP ban.

4. Chat Conduct ("Don't Be Mean")

  • Hate Speech: We do not tolerate racism, sexism, homophobia, or extreme harassment. Trash talking is fine, hate is not.
  • Real-Life Threats & Doxxing: Threatening someone's real-life safety or sharing personal info is a permanent ban.
  • Spam & Advertising: Do not spam the chat or advertise other servers/links.
