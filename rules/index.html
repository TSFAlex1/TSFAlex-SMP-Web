Server Rules

Please read these carefully. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.

1. General Conduct

  • Be Respectful: Harassment, hate speech, sexism, racism, or severe toxicity will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or personal links in chat.
  • Keep Chat Clean: Spamming, excessive caps, or starting massive arguments in global chat is not allowed.

2. Gameplay & Modifications

  • No Cheating or Hacking: X-Ray, fly hacks, combat modifications (killaura), or any client that gives an unfair advantage are strictly forbidden.
  • Approved Mods: Optifine, Sodium, minimaps (without player/cave tracking), and schematic mods (without printer) are allowed.
  • Exploits: Duplication glitches (except TNT/Carpet duping, if approved by staff) and server-crashing exploits are bannable.

3. Griefing & Raiding

  • No Griefing: Destroying or altering another player's build without their permission is prohibited.
  • No Stealing: Taking items from chests, shulkers, or barrels that do not belong to you is not allowed. All chests are logged.
  • PvP Restrictions: Player vs. Player combat is only allowed if both parties agree to it beforehand. No random killing.
